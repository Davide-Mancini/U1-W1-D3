Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste budsd with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramellized onions,tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It wiil become your new favorite holiday starter

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes (scrape rinf off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh of frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment,mix together all the dough ingredients until flour is fully absorbed.You don't nedd to actually knead the dough,tough i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
